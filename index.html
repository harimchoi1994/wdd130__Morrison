Have your Pizza and your diet too

Section One: Welcome to Zucchini Bros. Pizza: a classic pizzeria with a twist: All of our crust is made out of fresh zucchini! As gross as that sounds, this healthier option tastes just as good as normal pizza dough without any of the guilt. And this is coming from people who hate zucchini... Just like you!

Section Two: Our crust is always made out of fresh zucchini that is grown at our own farms. With the nasty juice squeezed out and thrown together with tasty ingredients like garlic and parmesan cheese, you'd never guess you were eating zucchini if it weren't written on the box.

Section Three: We have a variety of toppings to choose from, including the classics like pepperoni and sausage, as well as some more unique options like pineapple and jalapenos. We also have a variety of sauces to choose from, including our famous Zucchini Sauce, which is made from fresh zucchini grown at our farms.
