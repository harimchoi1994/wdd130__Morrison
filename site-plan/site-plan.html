Overview

Purpose

This website is designed to help customers order pizza made of Zucchini crust, apply for jobs, show deals and upcoming additions to the menu, and to advertize for the hypothetical company I will one day start of the same name.

Audience

This website is specifically designed to help people who are dieting and still want to enjoy pizza. the Zucchini crust that I make is both healthy and delicious, and this is coming from someone that can't stand zucchini. So anyone that enjoys pizza but doesn't want to sacrifice their health goals can order from here without losing the great flavor of their favorite pizza.

Content

Home page

Have your pizza and your diet too.

Section One: Welcome to Zucchini Bros. Pizza: a classic pizzaria with a twist: All of our crust is made out of fresh zucchini! As gross as that sounds, this healthier option tastes just as good as normal pizza dough without any of the guilt. And this is coming from people who hate zucchini... Just like you!

Section 2: Our crust is always made out of fresh zucchini that is grown at our own farms. with the nasty juice squeezed out and thrown together with tasty ingredients like garlic and parmasean cheese, you'd never guess you were eating Zucchini if it weren't written on the box.

Section Three: While other pizzarias leave you feeling bloated and guilty, we guarentee that our zucchini crust pizza will leave you satisfied and refreshed, or your money back guarenteed. Give Zucchini Bros. a chance today, and prepare to make zucchini your new favorite food!

Jobs

We need your help!

Section one: Zucchini Bros. offers a wide variety of job opportunities! Whether you're entering the work force, a student in need of work, or just looking to earn some extra money, we have lots of opportunities for work, and we'd love to have you on our team!

Section 2: Pizza Chef: If you enjoy cooking and trying out new recipes on the side, then consider working on our product line as a pizza chef! You'll get to learn all about how we make our secret Zucchini crust, and all of our other incredible recipes too! If you like to cook, our makeline will be perfect for you!

Section Three: Cashier: Like working up front where the action is? Then consider applying to work as a cashier! As the face of our store, you'll have lots of opportunities to meet new people and introduce them to the concept of vegetables tasting good for the very first time! If you have great people skills, then you'd be perfect as a cashier for Zucchini Bros.!

Section Four: Delivery Driver: Enjoy driving? Who doesn't? As a delivery driver, you can get paid for driving around town listening to your favorite music, sitting in a car that smells like fresh pizza! What could possibly be better than that?
